Why I love Lana Del Rey

Over the past couple years Lana Del Rey (formerly known as Elizabeth Grant, Lizzie Grant, etc) has become my favorite artist. Lana Del Rey is extremley talented and an artist I can listen to no matter my mood. You may still be wondering, "why does she love Lana Del Rey so much?", I'll tell you why:

  • Lana Del Rey has such a unique voice. She has certain vocal qualities of crooners and singers of the mid century, yet she also shifts her voice and uses more modern technique.
  • Lana Del Rey is an incredible lyricist. She creates beautiful images with her lyrics, and truly builds out the world in her songs. When listening to her songs you truly feel like you're there with her.
  • Her music videos are unbelievable. While many of her videos are remarkable, Lana Del Rey's video for her song "National Anthem" is unbeat. The video stars A$AP Rocky as JFK, and Lana Del Rey playing both Marilyn Monroe and Jackie Onassis. The video is beautifully shot, and really captures Del Rey's vintage-Americana aesthetic.
  • So much of Lana Del Rey's personally life is a mystery, the biggest thing we have of her is her music. Because of her enigma-like prescense her music is able to really belong with her audience instead of "who it might be about".
